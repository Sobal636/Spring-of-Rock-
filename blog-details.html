Abu Dhabi, Successful Completion of Water Bottling Project at Arjan Park by Rotana, Khalifa Park.

At Spring of Rock General Maintenance Contracting, we are proud to announce the successful completion of our latest project at Arjan Park by Rotana, located in Khalifa Park, Abu Dhabi. This water bottling project involved a comprehensive range of services, showcasing our expertise in civil, electrical, plumbing, and waterproofing works.

Our scope of work included the installation of floor and wall tiles, complete waterproofing solutions, and concrete work to ensure the durability and reliability of the structure. Additionally, we installed PVC and PPR hot, cold, and return lines, as well as drain lines, to support the plumbing infrastructure. From the electrical standpoint, we carried out SMDB to DB connections with precision, ensuring a safe and reliable electrical setup.

A new push-in type wall ceiling was installed, adding to the overall functionality and aesthetic appeal of the facility.

At Spring of Rock General Maintenance Contracting, we take pride in delivering high-quality results on time and within budget. Our team's dedication to excellence has once again proven that we can handle complex, multidisciplinary projects, ensuring the complete satisfaction of our clients.

For inquiries or to learn more about our services, visit our website or contact us today!

We have a large network of nartners and subcontractors who work under our responsibility and control in addition to our ultra-modern fleet.
