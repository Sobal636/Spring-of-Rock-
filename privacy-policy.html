Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is a pivotal agreement between a company and its clientele, ensuring consistent maintenance and support services over a specified period. Opting for an AMC offers numerous advantages, primarily guaranteeing that your systems remain updated and operate seamlessly. Moreover, it grants peace of mind, knowing that reliable assistance is readily available should any issues arise. Spring of Rock General Maintenance and Contracting Company specializes in offering comprehensive and non-comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs) tailored for both residential and commercial properties across Abu Dhabi and the UAE. Our AMC services encompass an extensive array of maintenance and repair solutions, including AC/HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing, Civil, Carpentry, Housekeeping, and more. Additionally, we provide one-time general maintenance and installation services for HVAC/AC, Electrical, Plumbing, Civil, Carpentry, Painting, as well as all types of pit-out contracts and Housekeeping services.

Benefits of an Annual Maintenance Contract:

1. Peace of Mind: Regular servicing by experts reduces the likelihood of breakdowns, allowing you to focus on your business with peace of mind.

2. Cost Savings: An AMC can prevent costly downtime by detecting and addressing problems early, ultimately saving you money in the long run.

3. Enhanced Equipment Efficiency: : Routine maintenance ensures that your equipment operates at peak efficiency, leading to savings on energy bills.

4. Extended Equipment Lifespan: Regular maintenance helps catch and address small issues before they escalate, ultimately extending the lifespan of your equipment.

5. Safety Assurance: An AMC ensures that your equipment is always equipped with the latest safety features and compliant with industry standards, enhancing overall safety in your establishment.
